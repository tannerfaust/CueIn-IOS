Concept and General Idea:

It’s an app that lets you structure your life through customizable “formulas” — predefined customisable schedules for your days, that define how your time should be spent. Instead of just listing tasks, you build a system where your time blocks, activities, and goals are connected. As you follow these formulas, tracking our time in a conviniet way, the app tracks what you actually do, quantifies your progress across different areas of life, and allows you to continuously refine and improve your structure. In essence, it treats your life like a product — something you design, measure, and iterate on — so you can progressively optimize how you spend your time and move toward your goals full potential in general.
